Our team operates at the [Quadram Institute Bioscience](https://www.quadram.ac.uk),
a research institute in Norwich, UK. 

We are part of the **[Core Bioinformatics group](https://quadram.ac.uk/support_groups/core-bioinformatics/)**,
which maintains both hardware and software infrastructure to support the research in microbial genomics.
