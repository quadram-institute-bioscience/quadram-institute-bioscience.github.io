We are delighted to open applications for the **[BioFAIR workshop](https://dimmestp.github.io/FAIR4FacilitiesWorkshop/)**, focusing on the barriers and opportunities in producing FAIR data and workflows within UK life science facilities.

<!--more-->

Join us for a two-day event centered on facilitated discussions about current FAIR (Findable, Accessible, Interoperable, and Reusable) practices. We’ll be highlighting key obstacles and mapping out a community roadmap to make FAIR data a reality across UK institutes.

### Who is this for?
If you work at a facility within a UK life science institute, we want to hear from you! Whether you are a technician producing data, a bioinformatician analyzing it, a data steward managing it, or a facility manager, your perspective is vital. FAIR experts and newcomers are equally welcome.

### Key Info
*   📅 **When:** Thursday 9th July (12:00) – Friday 10th July (16:00)
*   📍 **Where:** Quadram Institute, Norwich
*   ✨ **Cost:** Free! Accommodation and catering are covered thanks to Quadram and BioFAIR. 
*   🚗 **Bursaries:** A small travel bursary is available for those in need.
