We are pleased to announce that **Lisa Marchioretto** and **Andrea Telatin** 
are attending [BITS 2026](https://bioinformatics.it/bits2026) — the 22nd annual conference of the 
[Italian Bioinformatics Society](https://bioinformatics.it/).

<!--more-->

Both researchers are presenting their work as posters at the conference:

- A poster on [CLIMB](https://www.climb.ac.uk) presented by Lisa Marchioretto — [Download poster (PDF)](https://github.com/quadram-institute-bioscience/quadram-institute-bioscience.github.io/releases/download/v2026.1/2026-BITS-Marchioretto-Poster.pdf)
- A poster on [BamToCov](https://telatin.github.io/bamtocov), presented by Andrea Telatin — [Download poster (PDF)](https://github.com/quadram-institute-bioscience/quadram-institute-bioscience.github.io/releases/download/v2026.1/2026-bits-telatin-binfi.pdf)
