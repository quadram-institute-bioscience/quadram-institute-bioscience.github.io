Andrea Telatin and Evelien Adriaenssens travelled to Brest, France, to deliver a viromics workshop at the **9th EBAME Workshop on Computational Microbial Ecogenomics** (October 12–26, 2024).

<!--more-->

![EBAME 2024]({{ site.baseurl }}/images/photos/ebame-2024.jpg)

EBAME brings together researchers generating complex 'omics datasets with developers of new computational methods, covering genome-resolved metagenomics, micro-diversity analysis, strain deconvolution, comparative genomics, and data visualisation.

Andrea and Evelien contributed a hands-on viromics session as part of the workshop's focus on state-of-the-art ecogenomics approaches.
